About

My journey into AI began in 2019, evolving from a fascination with cognitive architectures to a deep expertise in reinforcement learning and MLOps. This dual passion led me to pursue master's degrees in both Artificial Intelligence and Organizational Psychology, giving me a unique perspective that blends technical algorithm design with a profound understanding of human cognition.

Currently, as a GenAI Solutions Lead at ABN-AMRO's CISO, I'm at the forefront of applying advanced AI to solve critical cybersecurity challenges. I architect and deploy secure, scalable Generative AI systems to detect sophisticated threats, from advanced phishing campaigns to automated secret scanning in vast codebases.

My core mission is to translate state-of-the-art research into tangible, high-impact solutions, engineering AI systems that are not just intelligent but also robust, reliable, and fundamentally secure.
